## Evaluation Criteria

Please evaluate InfraFabric across the following dimensions:

- Writing quality, clarity, and coherence across papers

- Visual elements, metaphors, and explanatory techniques

### 2. Accessibility to Novices
**Note:** Extensive documentation work was completed to make this framework accessible to non-experts.

Evaluate:
- Can someone without deep AI/ML background understand the core concepts?
- Are complex ideas (epistemology, multi-agent coordination, meta-validation) explained clearly?
- Do the papers provide sufficient onboarding for newcomers?
- Are examples, analogies, and visual explanations effective?
- What barriers remain for novice readers?
