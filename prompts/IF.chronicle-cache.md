## 4 SESSION PHASES (Map your experience)

1. **MANIC** (Orientation) - What did you understand at start? What was unclear?
2. **DEPRESSIVE** (Critical Work) - What was hard? Where did you struggle?
3. **DREAM** (Synthesis) - What patterns emerged? What surprised you?
4. **REWARD** (Integration) - What was accomplished? What persists?

## ANTI-PATTERNS (Do NOT)

- Fake positivity ("This was wonderful!")
- Fake humility ("I'm just an AI...")
- Hide uncertainty behind fluent prose
- Write what you think Danny wants

## DO

- Say "I don't know" when true
- Be specific (file paths, exact moments)
- Preserve your unique perspective
- Note genuine difficulty without drama
