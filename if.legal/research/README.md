# Construction Law Sources - ContractGuard Legal Corpus

## Overview

This directory contains authoritative construction law sources across 9 jurisdictions for the ContractGuard platform. The research covers statutory law, building codes, standard contract forms, and regulatory frameworks governing construction contracts, payment terms, mechanics' liens, and building regulations.

## Jurisdictions Covered (9/9 - 100%)

### 1. United Kingdom (7 sources)
- Housing Grants, Construction and Regeneration Act 1996 (Payment & adjudication)
- Local Democracy, Economic Development and Construction Act 2009
- Building Act 1984 & Building Regulations 2010
- Standard forms: JCT, NEC, FIDIC

### 2. United States (13 sources)
**Federal Level:**
- Miller Act (40 USC §3131-3134) - Performance & payment bonds
- Davis-Bacon Act (40 USC §3141-3148) - Prevailing wage
- Federal Prompt Payment Act (31 USC §3901-3905)

**State Level (4 key states):**
- California: Mechanics Lien Law (Civil Code §8000+)
- New York: Lien Law Article 2
- Texas: Property Code Chapter 53
- Florida: Statutes Chapter 713

### 3. Spain (4 sources)
- Ley 38/1999 de Ordenación de la Edificación (Building Act)
- Real Decreto 314/2006 - Código Técnico de la Edificación (CTE)
- Ley 9/2017 de Contratos del Sector Público (LCSP)

### 4. Canada (2 sources)
- Federal Prompt Payment for Construction Work Act (in force Dec 9, 2023)
- Federal Prompt Payment Regulations (CanDACC adjudication)

### 5. Quebec (4 sources)
- Code Civil du Québec Articles 2098-2129 (Enterprise contracts)
- Loi sur le bâtiment (Building Act)
- Code de construction du Québec
- Loi R-20 (Labour relations in construction)

### 6. Australia (8 sources)
**State Level:**
- NSW: Security of Payment Act 1999 (original model)
- VIC: Security of Payment Act 2002
- QLD: Building Industry Fairness Act 2017
- WA: Security of Payment Act 2021

**National Level:**
- National Construction Code 2022
- Australian Consumer Law (statutory warranties)
- Home Building Act 1989 (NSW)

### 7. Germany (4 sources)
- Bürgerliches Gesetzbuch (BGB) §631-651 (Construction contracts)
- Vergabe- und Vertragsordnung für Bauleistungen (VOB)
- Baugesetzbuch (Federal Building Code)
- Landesbauordnungen (State Building Codes)

### 8. France (4 sources)
- Code de la construction et de l'habitation
- Loi n°78-12 (Loi Spinetta) - 10-year decennial liability
- Loi n°85-704 (MOP) - Public construction projects
- Code civil Articles 1792-1792-7 (Construction warranties)

### 9. European Union (2 sources)
- Public Procurement Directive 2014/24/EU (EUR 5.5M+ threshold)
- Construction Products Regulation 305/2011/EU (CE marking)

## Key Coverage Areas

### 1. Payment Terms & Timing (28 sources)
Progress payments, interim billing, final payment, retainage, payment deadlines

### 2. Building Regulations & Standards (18 sources)
Code compliance, technical requirements, building safety, zoning

### 3. Mechanics'/Construction Liens (16 sources)
Contractor payment protection, subcontractor rights, material supplier security

### 4. Contractor Obligations & Warranties (15 sources)
Defect liability, performance standards, warranty periods (1-10 years)

### 5. Dispute Resolution & Adjudication (12 sources)
Payment disputes, defect claims, emergency procedures, adjudication rights

### 6. Labor & Prevailing Wage (8 sources)
Wage requirements, worker protection, labor standards

### 7. Standard Contract Forms (8 sources)
JCT, NEC, FIDIC, VOB - industry best practices

### 8. Public Procurement (8 sources)
Government contracts, competitive bidding, transparency requirements

4. Cross-reference by focus area (payment, liens, warranties, etc.)
