## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Search Strategy Used
1. **Direct statutory searches** - Official government legislation databases
2. **Federal/national level** - Primary construction/contracts statutes
3. **State/provincial level** - Key states (CA, NY, TX, FL for USA; NSW, VIC, QLD, WA for Australia)
4. **Building codes** - National and state/regional technical standards
5. **Standard forms** - Industry-standard contracts (JCT, NEC, FIDIC, VOB)
6. **Labor/payment regulations** - Prompt payment, prevailing wage, lien laws

### Coverage Summary
- **Total Sources**: 58 authoritative sources
- **Jurisdictions**: 9 (UK, USA-Federal, USA-States, Spain, Canada, Quebec, Australia, Germany, France, EU)
- **Document Types**: Acts, Statutes, Royal Decrees, Directives, Regulations, Standard Forms, Building Codes
- **Primary Focus Areas**:
  - Contract formation and interpretation
  - Payment terms and timing
  - Mechanics'/Construction liens
  - Building regulations and standards
  - Labor laws and prevailing wage
  - Adjudication and dispute resolution
  - Warranties and defects liability

### Prioritization
- **P0 (Critical)**: Core construction laws governing contracts, payment, liens, building standards
- **P1 (Important)**: Supporting statutes, related laws, important case law databases
- **P2 (Reference)**: Model laws, comparative frameworks, optional secondary sources
