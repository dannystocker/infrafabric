# Quebec Comprehensive Legal Sources for ContractGuard

## Overview

This comprehensive legal corpus covers Quebec-specific legislation across nine legal verticals. Quebec operates under a civil law system (distinct from common law in the rest of Canada) and has a unique legal framework based on the Code Civil du Québec. The Compilation des lois et règlements du Québec (CQLR) provides the official codification.

**Primary Sources:**
- **LégisQuébec**: https://www.legisquebec.gouv.qc.ca/ (official Quebec legislation portal)
- **CanLII Quebec**: https://www.canlii.org/en/qc/legis/ (Canadian Legal Information Institute)
- **Publications du Québec**: https://www.publicationsduquebec.gouv.qc.ca/ (official publisher)

## KEY DOCUMENTS FOR CONTRACTGUARD IMPLEMENTATION

### Critical Civil Law Foundation
1. **Code Civil du Québec (CQLR c CCQ-1991)** - The foundation of all Quebec private law. Books IV and V cover property and obligations/contracts essential for legal automation.
   - Book IV (Property): Articles 871-1370
   - Book V (Obligations): Articles 1371-1641 (contracts, lease, sales)

### Employment Contract Framework
1. **Loi sur les normes du travail (c N-1.1)** - Establishes minimum conditions that override freedom of contract
2. **Charte des droits et libertés de la personne (c C-12)** - Non-waivable protections
3. **Loi sur l'équité salariale (c E-12.001)** - Mandatory pay equity audits for employers with 10+ employees

### Commercial Contract Framework
1. **Civil Code Book V** - Contract formation, interpretation, effects
2. **Business Corporations Act (c S-31.1)** and **Companies Act (c C-38)** - Corporate authority to contract
3. **Act respecting the legal publicity of enterprises (c P-44.1)** - Validation of business registration

### Real Property & Leasing Framework
1. **Civil Code Articles 1851-2000** - Lease framework (residential and commercial)
2. **Act respecting the Régie du logement (c R-8.1)** - Residential lease dispute resolution
3. **Regulation on Land Publication (c CCQ, r 6)** - Real property registration

### Risk Management Framework
1. **Act respecting insurance (c A-32)** - Insurance contract regulation
2. **Act respecting the distribution of financial products and services (c D-9.2)** - Professional standards for advisors
