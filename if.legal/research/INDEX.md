### Essential Foundations for ContractGuard

**Core Civil Law:**
- Code Civil du Québec (CQLR c CCQ-1991) - Foundation of all private law
- Loi sur les normes du travail (CQLR c N-1.1) - Non-waivable employment minimums
- Charte des droits et libertés de la personne (CQLR c C-12) - Human rights protections

**Key Administrative Frameworks:**
- CNESST: Labour standards, workers' compensation, pay equity
- TAT: Labour disputes and collective bargaining
- TAL: Residential tenancy disputes
- AMF: Insurance and financial services regulation
- RBQ/CCQ: Construction standards and labour relations

## HOW TO USE THIS CORPUS

### For ContractGuard Implementation

1. **Contract Validation:** Use civil law sources (Code Civil Book V) to validate contract formation and enforceability
2. **Compliance Checking:** Map clauses against non-waivable protections (employment standards, tenant rights, human rights)
3. **Jurisdiction Routing:** Route disputes to appropriate forums (TAT, TAL, Superior Court, etc.)
4. **Bilingual Support:** Leverage official bilingual texts for French/English contract generation
5. **Regulatory Integration:** Connect to administrative agency requirements (pay equity audits, building codes, etc.)
