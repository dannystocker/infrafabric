### Framework Characteristics

**Unbiased ("Blind Test"):**
- Evaluators assess without being told if document is "good" or "bad"
- Pure objectivity, no pre-bias
- Reproducible (same evaluator gets consistent results)

**7 Evaluation Dimensions:**
1. Conceptual Quality (0-10) — Is logic sound?
2. Technical Feasibility (0-10) — Can it be built?
3. Strategic Positioning (0-10) — Durable competitive advantage?
4. Market Fit (0-10) — Will clients want it?
5. Operational Practicality (0-10) — Can team execute?
6. Risk Assessment (0-10) — Risks identified & mitigated?
7. Client Readiness (0-10) — Ready for client tomorrow?

**Output Format:**
- YAML-structured (for automated diff/merge across evaluators)
- Includes: scores, justifications, strengths/weaknesses, evidence, red flags, golden moments
- Designed for multi-LLM aggregation (5 independent assessments)

**Decision Framework:**
- Mean score ≥8.5: Ready for immediate client presentation
- Mean score 7.0-8.4: Ready with minor cleanup (1-2 days)
- Mean score 5.5-6.9: Ready with revisions (3-7 days)
- Mean score 4.0-5.4: Major rework needed
- Mean score <4.0: Return to drawing board
