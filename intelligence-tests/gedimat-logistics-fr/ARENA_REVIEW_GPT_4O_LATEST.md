## 1. IF.TTT Compliance: PASS
**Score:** 98/100
**Unsourced Gedimat claims found:** 0

**Verdict:** EXCELLENT. The dossier demonstrates exceptional adherence to IF.TTT (Traceable, Transparent, Trustworthy) methodology. Every financial or operational projection is either:
1. Presented as a formula with placeholders for real data
2. Explicitly labeled as a hypothesis requiring validation
3. Defined as an observable process metric

This approach eliminates "phantom numbers" entirely. The PDG can confidently present this document knowing that no claim will be challenged as unsourced.

**Examples of IF.TTT excellence:**
- `RSI = [Baseline factures 30j] / [Investissement] × [8-15%]` → Formula awaiting real data
- "Application hypothétique Gedimat (À VALIDER avec données réelles)" → Clear hypothesis labeling
- "% of orders assigned by proximity score" → Observable metric, not projection

**Why this matters:** Standard consulting deliverables often include impressive-sounding numbers ("€2M annual savings!") based on opaque models or unverifiable assumptions. These "phantom numbers" create credibility risk when the CFO asks "where did this number come from?" This dossier's formula-first approach invites collaboration ("let's plug in *our* numbers") rather than skepticism.

**Minor deduction:** A few sections could benefit from even more explicit data requirement statements. For example, Annexe Z's cost model could include a bullet point: "IMPORTANT : Ces montants sont des exemples illustratifs. Ils seront remplacés par les données Médiafret réelles lors du pilote Semaine 1."
