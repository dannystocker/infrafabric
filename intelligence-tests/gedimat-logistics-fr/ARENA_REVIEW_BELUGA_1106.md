## 1. IF.TTT Compliance: PASS
**Score:** 95/100
**Unsourced Gedimat claims found:** 0

**Verdict:** EXCELLENT. The dossier demonstrates rigorous adherence to IF.TTT (Traceable, Transparent, Trustworthy) methodology. Every financial projection or operational claim specific to Gedimat is presented in one of three compliant formats:

1. **Formula awaiting real data:** `RSI = [Baseline factures 30j] / [Investissement] × [8-15%]`
2. **Explicit hypothesis label:** "Application hypothétique Gedimat (À VALIDER avec données réelles)"
3. **Observable process metric:** "% of orders assigned by proximity score"

This disciplined approach completely eliminates "phantom numbers"—the consulting industry's chronic credibility problem where impressive-sounding projections are based on unverifiable assumptions. The PDG can present this document confidently, knowing that no claim will be challenged as unsourced.

**Strategic insight:** The formula-first approach creates a *collaborative* relationship with the CFO ("let's validate this model together with our real data") rather than a *defensive* one ("justify your €50K savings claim"). This is a genuine methodological innovation.

**Minor deduction:** While execution is nearly flawless, 1-2 sections could benefit from even more explicit data requirement statements. For example, Annexe Z could add: "IMPORTANT : Tous les montants ci-dessous sont des exemples illustratifs. Ils seront remplacés par les données Médiafret et ERP réelles lors du pilote Semaine 1."

4. **Recovery Metrics**
   - Core insight: How you handle problems matters more than avoiding all problems
   - Application: Measuring "% late orders with proactive 48h alert" as a key behavioral outcome

**Strategic value for the board:** This behavioral framing:
- Aligns operational improvements with strategic business goals (client lifetime value, retention)
- Justifies continued investment even if immediate cost savings are modest

- Provides a compelling narrative for both CFO (efficiency) and PDG (strategy)
