## 5. French Language Quality: CONDITIONAL
**Score:** 85/100
**Anglicisms Section 1:** 3
**Anglicisms total:** 7 ("Quick Win/Quick Wins" appears 3 times, "ROI" 2 times mixed with "RSI", "dashboard" 1 time, "benchmark" 1 time)
**Tone:** Professional and appropriate for C-suite audience

**Verdict:** The French is generally excellent and C-suite appropriate. However, the presence of anglicisms is the most consistent weakness across the document. For a board-level French document, these should all be eliminated:

**Required replacements:**
- "Quick Win" → "Gain Rapide"
- "ROI" → "RSI" (consistently)
- "dashboard" → "tableau de bord"
- "benchmark" → "référence externe" or "point de comparaison"
- "KPI" → "ICP" (Indicateur Clé de Performance)

With these changes, the document would be linguistically flawless for a French board presentation.
