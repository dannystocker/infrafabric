## Why 2 Layers (Not 4)?

**IF.guard Council Decision (71.4% consensus):**

**Time Constraint:**
- 4 layers = 1,800 words/section √ó 21 = 37,800 words = 40 hours
- 2 layers = 600 words/section √ó 21 = 12,600 words = 8-10 hours = **FEASIBLE**

**Value Equivalence:**
- V3.2 = Single audience (board only)
- V3.3 (2-layer) = Dual audience (board + ALL non-board)
- 4 layers adds 3 sub-audiences (C-Suite/Operational/Political) = **marginal value vs. 3√ó time cost**

**Cultural Fit:**
- French business formality = 2 layers (respect + accessibility)
- 4 layers = Silicon Valley tech style (cultural mismatch)

**Testability:**
- 2 layers = Simple hypothesis: "Does simple French improve understanding?" (testable via Arena)
- 4 layers = Multiple untestable claims (+30% approval, -60% questions, -50% resistance)
