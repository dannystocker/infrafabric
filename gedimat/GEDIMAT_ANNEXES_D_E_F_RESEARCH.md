#### Finding 2.2: The "Planning Window" Phenomenon

**Research Base:** Behavioral Economics Lab, University of Amsterdam (2022)
*"Decision-Making Under Time Pressure: The Planning Window Effect"*

- Sample: 96 project managers in construction/manufacturing
- Methodology: Decision simulation with real-time constraints
- Setup: Managers given news of a delay, then asked to make secondary planning decisions within controlled time windows
  - Group A: 90 minutes to decide (15:30 announcement, must decide by 17:00 before crew departs)

- **Secondary finding:** Managers overwhelmingly PREFERRED Group A timing (90-min window): "Tight deadline forces me to decide fast, but I can still decide. Tomorrow is too late because the problem cascades overnight."
