## FACET 2: RORY THE BEHAVIORAL ECONOMIST

## SYNTHESIS: THE SUTHERLAND METHOD FOR COUPLES THERAPY

### THE 5 SUTHERLAND PRINCIPLES FOR THERAPEUTIC REFRAMING

#### 1. **REFRAME FROM DEFICIT TO DESIGN**

Don't ask: "What's broken?"

#### 2. **INTERVIEW BEHAVIOR, NOT BELIEFS**

Don't ask: "Why do you think you feel this way?"

#### 3. **EXTERNALIZE THE PROBLEM**

Don't ask: "What's wrong with you/your partner?"
Ask: "If this relationship were a product/brand/system, what would need redesigning?"

#### 4. **OPTIMIZE FOR REVEALED PREFERENCE, NOT STATED VALUES**

Don't ask: "What do you want?"
Ask: "What did you actually do with your last free hour?"

#### 5. **REDUCE FRICTION, DON'T INCREASE WILLPOWER**

Don't ask: "How can you try harder?"
